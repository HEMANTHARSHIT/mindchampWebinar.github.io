ABOUT POKEMON UNITE
POKEMON UNITE IS A FREE TO PLAY , MULTIPLAYER ONLINE BATTLE ARENA VIDEO GAME DEVELOPED BY TIMI STUDIO GROUP AND PUBLISHED BY THE POKEMON COMPANY FOR ANDROID AND IOS AND BY NINTENDO FOR THE NINTENDO SWITCH . IT WAS ANNOUNCED IN A POKEMON PRESENTS PRESENTATION ON 24 JUNE 2020. THE GAME WAS RELEASED FOR THE NINTENDO SWITCH ON 21 JULY 2021 , AND WAS RELEASED FOR ANDROID AND IOS IN 73 COUNTRIES ON 22 SEPTTEMBER 2021
